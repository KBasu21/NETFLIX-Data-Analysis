CONCLUSION

Q1. What is the most frequent genre of movies released on Netflix?

In the given dataset, the most frequent genre of movies that released on Netflix is Drama with 3744 movies.

Q2. Which has highest votes in vote avg column?

Kung Fu Master Huo Yuanjia, released on 09 April 2020, has the highest votes, i.e., 10.0, in Vote_Average column

Q3. What movie has got the highest popularity? What's its genre?

Spider-Man: No Way Home, released on 15 December 2021, is a Action, Adventure, Science Fiction movie, which is the most popular movie in Netflix.

Q4. What movie has got the lowest popularity? What's its genre?

There are two movies which got the lowest popularity in Netflix:-

  1. The United States vs. Billie Holiday, released on 31 March 2021
  2. Threads, released on 23 September 1984

Q5. Which year has the most filmmed movies?

In 2021, 714 movies got released on Netflix, making it the most filmmed year.
